## Our Commitment

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

## What We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Name and Email:** When you contact us or sign up for updates
- **Messages:** Any information you include in contact forms or emails
- **Company Information:** If you reach out for business inquiries

### Information We Collect Automatically
- **IP Address:** For security, abuse prevention, and analytics
- **Browser Information:** Basic technical data (browser type, device type)
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent (if analytics enabled)

## Why We Collect This Information

**Contact & Communication:**
- To respond to your inquiries
- To send updates about our products (only if you opt in)
- To notify early access participants when we launch

**Security & Abuse Prevention:**
- To prevent spam and malicious activity
- To protect our website and users
- To comply with legal requirements

**Analytics:**
- To understand how people use our site
- To improve user experience
- To make informed product decisions

## How We Store Your Information

- **Secure Servers:** Your data is stored on secure servers in India
- **No Selling:** We never sell your personal information to third parties
- **No Sharing:** We don't share your data except:
  - When required by law
  - With service providers who help us operate (email, hosting)
  - With your explicit consent

**Service Providers We May Use:**
- Email service providers (for contact and updates)
- Website hosting providers
- Analytics tools (anonymized where possible)

You have the right to:

**Access:** Request a copy of your data we hold  
**Correction:** Request corrections to inaccurate data  
**Deletion:** Request deletion of your data  
**Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from communications at any time

## Data Retention

- **Contact Information:** Retained until you request deletion
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized after 24 months

Our website is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it immediately.
